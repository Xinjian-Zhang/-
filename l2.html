  • Req of UT SIS

    What are the requirements of the UT students for the study info system (SIS)? [P15]

    User-friendly and intuitive: Students often want a system interface that is clear and easy to navigate, allowing them to quickly find the information they need.

    Course management functions: including viewing course schedules, registering for courses, viewing grades and credits, etc.

    Notification and reminder system: The system should be able to provide relevant notifications such as exams, assignment deadlines, important academic events, etc.

    Mobile device compatibility: Students may want to access the system through mobile devices such as mobile phones, so the system should support mobile access.

    Personalization: Students may wish to adjust the interface layout or set reminder options to suit their personal needs.

    Security and Privacy Protection: Keep student information secure and prevent unauthorized access.

    Effective support and help services: The system should provide user manuals, FAQs, online help or customer service support to solve user problems.

    Efficient performance and reliability: The system should have fast response time and high availability to reduce system failures and maintenance time.
